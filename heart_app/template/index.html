Feature 1


Age

Gender


 : 1 = Male, 0 = Female


03

Feature 3


chest pain type


 : 1 = Typical angina , 2 = atypical angina, 3 = non-anginal pain ,4 = asymptomatic .

04

Feature4


Resting blood pressure


 : Between 90/60mmHg and 120/80mmHg.

05

Feature5


serum cholestoral


  :Age Dependent (measured in mg/dl) .

06

Feature6


fasting blood sugar


 : > 120 mg/dl(Binary)(1 = true; 0 = false).

07

Feature7


resting ECG results


    (values 0,1,2).
 

08

Feature8


maximum heart rate


  Subtract the age from 220.
 

09

Feature9


exercise induced angina


  : (1 = yes; 0 = no).
 

10

Feature10


ST depression


 : induced by exercise relative to  rest .
 

11

Feature11


(slope) of the ST segment


  (Value 1: upsloping , Value 2: flat , Value 3: downsloping ).

12

Feature12


number of major vessels


 (0-3, Ordinal) colored by fluoroscopy .

13

Feature13


maximum heart rate achieved


  (Ordinal): 3 = normal; 6 = fixed defect; 7 = reversable defect.
